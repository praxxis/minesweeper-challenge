Minesweeper! But not as you know it...

Instructions

The symbols on the board represent different game cell states.

o
Hidden A cell that has not yet been revealed.
f
Flagged A cell that may or may not contain a mine.
x
Mine A mine. If you see this, the game is over!

Revealing a cell will flag any adjacent mines. Hitting a mine will end the game. Flag all mines and press the "Validate" button to see if you've won.
